Who is K.Dot?

  • 1987- Kendrick Lamar Duckworth is born in Compton, California on June 17th

  • 1995- Kendrick visits filming of Tupac and Dr. Dre music video for "California Love," which quickly became an enormously influential event in Kendrick's life and music.

  • 2000- At the age of 13, Kendrick Lamar begins writing down raps

  • 2003- Kendrick Lamar, adopting the alias K-Dot, releases his first mixtape, Youngest Head Nigga in Charge.

  • 2003- After gaining some recognition and praise for his first mixtape, Kendrick Lamar signs a record deal with Top Dawg Entertainment, an independent label based in Carson, California.

  • 2009- Kendrick Lamar drops the pseudonym K-Dot and releases the Kendrick Lamar EP

  • 2012- Kendrick Lamar signs to Interscope Records and Dr. Dre’s Aftermath Entertainment. His following projects would be released jointly through these labels and Top Dawg Entertainment.

  • 2012- Kendrick Lamar releases his major label debut, good kid, m.A.A.d city, which sells 242,000 copies in its first week. The album is eventually certified platinum and receives 4 Grammy nominations including Album of the Year.

  • 2015- Kendrick Lamar’s 3rd album, To Pimp a Butterfly, is released one week ahead of schedule, breaking Spotify’s global first day streaming record (9.6 million).

  • 2016- Kendrick Lamar wins 5 Grammies! He wins: Best Rap Album of the Year - "To Pimp a Butterfly," Best Rap Song - "Alright," Best Rap/ Sung Collaboration - "These Walls," Best Rap Performance - "Alright," and Best Music Video - "Bad Blood."

  • 2016- Kendrick Lamar releases Untitled Unmastered, a compilation album of demos that originated from the recording of 2015’s To Pimp a Butterfly.

  • 2017- Kendrick Lamar releases his fourth studio album to critical acclaim.

  • 2018-Damn became the first non-classical and non-jazz album to be awarded the Pulitzer Prize for Music.
